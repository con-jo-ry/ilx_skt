sa utthāyāsanād ekāṃsam uttarāsaṅgaṃ kṛtvā dakṣiṇañ jānumaṇḍalaṃ pṛhivyāṃ pratiṣṭhāpya yena bhagavāṃs tenāñjaliṃ praṇamya bhagavantam etad avocat |
